History of The Sims Series

The Sims 1: Released on February 4, 2000. Created by Maxis, this game introduced players to the life simulation genre with groundbreaking concepts in virtual life management.

The Sims 2: Launched on September 14, 2004. It brought significant advancements with a new graphics engine, deeper simulation, and the introduction of genetics and aging.

The Sims 3: Released on June 2, 2009. Known for its open-world design, it expanded gameplay with more detailed customization and new social interactions.

The Sims 4: Released on September 2, 2014. It offers enhanced graphics, more interactive gameplay, and a continuous stream of expansions and updates.
